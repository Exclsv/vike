Notable use cases of Vike sponsors:

 - [BurdaForward](https://www.burda-forward.de) (large publishing house with online newspapers reaching over half of the German population), migrating their fragmented stacks across many news websites into one unified <Link href="/why#build-your-own-framework">company internal framework</Link> using Vike and Solid.
   ([Sponsoring the creator of Vike](https://github.com/orgs/burdaforward/sponsoring).)
 - [Alignable](https://www.alignable.com) (social network), using a bespoke integration of Ruby on {'Rails <=> Vike <=> React'}.
   ([Sponsoring the creator of Vike](https://github.com/AlignableUser?tab=sponsoring).)
 - [Contra](https://contra.com) (freelance market), large codebase (>150 routes) with a bespoke integration of {'React <=> Vike <=> GraphQL Relay'} with a custom rendering strategy of mixing SPA and SSR.
   ([Previously sponsoring  the creator of Vike](https://github.com/orgs/contra/sponsoring).)
 - [Ecosia](https://www.ecosia.org) (search engine), migrating their entire frontend from Nuxt to Vike for better performance and progressive migration from Vue 2 to Vue 3.
   ([Sponsoring Vike](https://github.com/Ecosia#:~:text=People-,Sponsoring,-Top%20languages).)
